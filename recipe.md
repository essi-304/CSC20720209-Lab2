# Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 6 tsp cinnamon
- 5 tbsp orange zest
- 4/5 cup dark chocolate
- hella vanilla
- probably a couple eggs

## Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
3. In another bowl whisk all the wet ingredients
4. Mix wet and dry ingredients together
5. Pour into a buttered cake shaped pan
6. Bake until it smells good and a toothpick is not gooey after sticking it in
